ABS — Abbreviations and Symbols
API — American Petroleum Institute
ASME — American Society of Mechanical Engineers
B31G — ASME B31G "Manual for Determining the Remaining Strength of Corroded Pipelines"
CGR — Corrosion Growth Rate (mm/yr)
COCL — Corrosion Cluster (POF 110)
CORR — Corrosion (POF 110)
DENT — Dent (POF 110)
DNV — Det Norske Veritas
ERF — Estimated Repair Factor (MAOP / Psafe; high ERF indicates lower safety margin)
FFP — Fitness-For-Purpose
ID — Inside Diameter
ILI — In-Line Inspection
LPG — Liquefied Petroleum Gas
MAOP — Maximum Allowable Operating Pressure
MFL — Magnetic Flux Leakage
OD — Outside Diameter
PINH — Pinhole (POF 110)
PITT — Pitting (POF 110)
POD — Probability of Detection (typically 10% WT for MFL-A tools at 80% confidence)
POF — Pipeline Operators Forum (specification 110 governs ILI data exchange)
Psafe — Safe Operating Pressure (kg/cm²)
RSTRENG — Remaining Strength (PRCI / Kiefner & Vieth, 1989)
SMYS — Specified Minimum Yield Strength (MPa)
SOP — Safe Operating Pressure (synonym for Psafe)
UTS — Ultimate Tensile Strength (MPa)
WT — Wall Thickness (mm)
